Tiny uppercase status pill; use for the NOW marker, copy-feedback, and small counts.

```jsx
<Badge tone="now">Now</Badge>
<Badge tone="soft">Tap to copy</Badge>
<Badge tone="success" icon="check">Copied</Badge>
```

- Tones: `now` (solid accent), `soft` (accent wash), `neutral` (surface), `success` (green).
